Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. In Italian cuisine it is made of stacked layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Products - crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonigs - fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. 1. Make the meat sauce
  2. 2. Cook the noodles
  3. 3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. 4. Layer the lasagna
  5. 5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. 6. Let lasagna rest before serving
